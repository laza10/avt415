P1.3 User Scenario

Scenario: Alyssa is building a new keyboard and wants to find affordable and clean purple keycaps that mesh well with her white board.

1) Alyssa browses the site to find keycaps she may like

a) Providing a variety of options in various colors

b) Providing keycaps in all purple or a mesh with other colors

2) She looks for purple keycaps that meet her needs

a) Offering keycaps in different shapes

b) Offering keycaps in different styles

3) She explores the other keycaps they offer on the site

a) Exploring the other colors and designs

b) Looks at designed keycaps compared to simple ones

c) Providing keycaps where the letters are visible through rgb or through a flat color

4) She considers the price of some of the purple keycaps

a) Offering prices that are more affordable but nice

b) Offering prices that are expensive but with amazing quality and color

5) Alyssa determines if she wants her keycaps to have designs or be simple

a) Showing options that are simply purple

b) Showing options that have designs

c) Having options where the keycaps are mixed with purple

6) She determines if she wants all her keycaps to be purple or have the main keys in a different color

a) Showing keycaps that have both purple and another color in the set

b) Showing keycaps in all purple

c) Showing keycaps in different shades of purple

d) Offering a ombre keycap option

7) She considers the reviews on the keys and the customer images

a) Looks at recent customer reviews

b) Considers the time frame customers have had it

c) Considers the quality of the keycaps from the reviews of customers

d) Looks at images that show customer usage if provided by the customer
